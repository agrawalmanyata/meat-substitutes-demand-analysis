# Meat Substitutes Demand Analysis

## Business Problem
This project analyzes the U.S. meat substitutes category for Conagra Brands to understand the factors influencing sales performance and category growth within the plant-based meat market. The objective was to identify opportunities related to pricing, product assortment, distribution, and competitive positioning using retail sales and product-level data across multiple years.

## Work Performed
The project involved data cleaning and preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, predictive modeling, pricing and elasticity analysis, assortment optimization, clustering analysis, and competitive benchmarking. Machine learning and statistical techniques were used to evaluate the impact of product attributes, pricing, and distribution on product sales and category performance.
